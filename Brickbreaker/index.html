    Controls

    Simple, fast, and meant to feel old-school.

    Move Use Left/Right, A/D, or drag on the board.
    Launch Press Space, Enter, or Start Game.
    Goal Break every brick without losing all your lives.
    Combo Each brick is worth 10 points times your multiplier. The multiplier resets when the ball hits the paddle.
    Count The Bricks stat tracks how many blocks you have broken this run.
    Drops Boosts can widen the paddle, slow the ball, or give one 30-second Bounce Back save.
    Flow Each cleared board increases the ball speed and advances the level.
